About Jemm

Local-first home automation.
No cloud, no tracking, no compromise.

Mission & Vision

We started Jemm because home automation had gone in the wrong direction. Too many systems depend on someone else's server, stop working when the internet goes down, and collect data you never agreed to share.

Your home should be yours.
That's what we're building.

Our Technology

Jemm Arc is built on proprietary inference technology developed in-house. From model training to local deployment, every layer of the stack is designed and maintained by our team.
