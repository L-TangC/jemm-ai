Local Processing

Your home's intelligence, inside your home. Jemm Arc runs all automation and voice processing on-site. No data leaves your home, no server in between, and fast, reliable control that works the way it should.

Voice Control

No matter where you are in the smart home world, Jemm is built for you. Whether you’re living in it, installing it for clients, or integrating our technology into your own product.

End-to-end voice processing, entirely on-device. Real-time response, no server dependency. Controls lights, temperature, security, and routines.

Configurable

Every home is different. Jemm works with your lights, sensors, schedules, and rules, then adapts your household’s routines and preferences into a system that feels personal, useful, and calm.

Offline Resilience

No internet, no problem. While other smart home systems stop working, Core keeps responding independently of Wi-Fi connectivity, allowing every local routine, voice command, and automation to continue.
